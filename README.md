## About Me

Research & Development Engineer at **[MSIS Lab](https://www.cbnu.msislab.com)**, Chungbuk National University, South Korea. I design scalable vision systems for **smart manufacturing** and **industrial visual inspection**, with a focus on **self-supervised learning** and **synthetic anomaly generation**.

Previously, I completed my **PhD in Electronics Engineering** at Chungbuk National University, where I developed deep learning frameworks for industrial anomaly detection — including **PAI-Lib** (pseudo-anomaly insertion) and **TASAD** (two-stage coarse-to-fine segmentation).
